# Video

Embed videos directly in your lessons — no YouTube links, no ads, no distractions. Videos are hosted via Mux with adaptive streaming quality.

---

## How It Works

1. Upload an `.mp4` or `.mov` file to your skript
2. Eduskript processes it with Mux for adaptive streaming
3. Reference it in markdown just like an image:

```markdown
![Explanation of the concept](my-video.mp4)
```

## Features

- **Adaptive quality**: Automatically adjusts to the student's connection speed
- **Progress tracking**: See how far each student has watched
- **Poster frames**: Auto-generated preview thumbnails
- **No external redirects**: Students stay on your page, fully focused

> [!tip] When to Use Video
> Video works best for:
> - Step-by-step walkthroughs of complex procedures
> - Lab demonstrations that can't be replicated digitally
> - Instructor introductions for online/hybrid courses
>
> For everything else, consider interactive elements (code editors, plugins) — they're more engaging than watching.
